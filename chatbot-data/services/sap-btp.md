- **Key Features**:

  - Formulate an actionable clean-core roadmap.
  - Simplify upgrade paths and lower technical debt.

## SAP BTP Application Development
- **Overview**: Build secure, custom applications and dashboards directly on SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP).
- **Benefits**: Protect your standard SAP core while creating customized business functions.

## SAP BTP Integration Suite

## SAP BTP Extension Architecture
