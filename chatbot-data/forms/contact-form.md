# Form Guide: Contact Us / Send a Message

## Purpose
Send a general query or message directly to our solution architects.

## Location
Found on the **Contact** page under the "Send us a Message" section.

## Fields
- **Full Name**: Enter your first and last name.
- **Email Address**: Enter your business email (e.g., john@company.com).
- **Phone Number**: Enter your contact number (e.g., +91 00000 00000).
- **Company Name**: Enter your corporate organization.
- **Your Message**: Describe your digital challenge or inquiry.

## Submission Timeline
One of our solution architects will contact you within **24 hours** of submission.

Please contact our team for further assistance.
