# Form Guide: Internship Application

## Purpose
Apply for a student internship or co-op program in AI Automation, SAP BTP, or Security.

## Location
Careers section / Internship details.

## Application Process
1. Go to the **Career** page.
2. Find the relevant Internship role (e.g., AI Automation Intern).
3. Click the **Apply Now** button.
4. This will redirect you to: https://calendly.com/anshiyainnovations/30min.
5. Book a short session to discuss your internship requirements and submit your details.
