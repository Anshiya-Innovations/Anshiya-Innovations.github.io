# Form Guide: Career Application

## Purpose
Apply for a full-time position as an SAP Security Analyst or SAP BTP Developer.

## Process Steps
1. Navigate to the **Career** page.
2. Under **Current Openings**, select your desired job role.
3. Click the **Apply Now** button on the job card.
4. You will be redirected to our portal/scheduler: https://calendly.com/anshiyainnovations/30min.
5. Book a session to review your qualifications with our team.
